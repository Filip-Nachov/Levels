Levels

Levels is a simple tier list creator built with vanilla JavaScript, CSS, and HTML.
It offers an intuitive way to organize and rank items based on your preferences.
With its user-friendly interface and minimalistic design,
Levels allows you to create and customize your tier lists easily,
making it a great tool for making tier lists without any limitations.
